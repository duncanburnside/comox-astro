About the Comox Valley Astronomy Club

The Comox Valley Astronomy Club is a non-profit society with the aim of promoting and enjoying amateur astronomy and related activities in the Comox Valley on Vancouver Island, British Columbia, Canada. Our club president is Francois Pilote. We meet at the Courtenay Public library on the first Monday of every month, and every Friday at 8pm PST at Vanier school field for observation sessions. For a complete schedule of meetings and observation times please see below.
